Grilled Cheese Sandwiches Recipe

Instruction

1. Mince the garlic cloves and cut the parsley.

2. Mix the minced garlic and parsley in melted butter. This will imbue the flavor of garlic to the butter and serve as a spread.

3. Cut the tomatoes, onions and peppers into dices.

4. Cut American Cheese and cheddar into slices.

5. Pan-fry the bacon stripes until crisps to create bacon fat. Do not have to oil the surface of the pan.

6. Remove bacon crisps from pan and spread garlic butter on 2 slices of bread.

7. Sear the bread until golden brown with bacon fat. Then cut the bacon into small pieces.

8. Place all of the ingredients on the bread slices and stack them altogether while in the pan.

9. After the cheese has melted, remove the sandwich from the pan.

10. Enjoy it as you like.
